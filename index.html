Recipe #1- pesto pizza.

  • Time: 20min
  • Serves: 6
1
Ingredients Quantity
pre-baked pizza crust (12 inch)
pesto ½ cup
ripe tomato, chopped 1
green bell pepper, chopped ½ cup
can chopped black olives, drained 1 (2 ounce)
small red onion, chopped ½
can artichoke hearts, drained and slice 1 (4 ounce)
crumbled feta cheese cup

Step 1: Preheat oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C)

Step 2: Spread pesto on pizza crust. Top with tomatoes, bell peppers, olives, red onions, artichoke hearts and feta cheese.

Step 3: Bake for 8 to 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and browned.

Recipe #2- Grilled Salmon.

  • Time:2 hrs 31 mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
pounds salmon fillets, 1 ½
lemon pepper to taste
garlic powder to taste
salt to taste
cup soy sauce, ⅓
cup brown sugar, ⅓
cup water, ⅓
cup vegetable oil ¼

Step 1: Season salmon fillets with lemon pepper, garlic powder, and salt.

Step 2: In a small bowl, stir together soy sauce, brown sugar, water, and vegetable oil until sugar is dissolved. Place fish in a large resealable plastic bag with the soy sauce mixture, seal, and turn to coat. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours.

Step 3: Preheat grill for medium heat.

Step 4: Lightly oil grill grate. Place salmon on the preheated grill, and discard marinade. Cook salmon for 6 to 8 minutes per side, or until the fish flakes easily with a fork.

Recipe #3- Lasagnaa.

  • Time:1 hr
  • Serves: 16
Ingredients Quantity
pounds ground beef, 1 ½
cup water, ¼
packet taco seasoning mix, 1 (1 ounce)
flour tortillas, or more if needed, 12 (8 inch)
can refried beans, 1 (14 ounce)
cups shredded Colby-Jack cheese, 3
jar taco sauce, 1 (8 ounce)
cup shredded Colby-Jack cheese, 1

Step 1: Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9x13-inch casserole dish.

Step 2: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and stir in ground beef. Cook and stir until beef is crumbly, evenly browned, and no longer pink, about 10 minutes. Drain and discard any excess grease. Add water and taco seasoning; cook and stir until mixture has thickened, 5 to 10 minutes.

Step 3: Arrange 3 tortillas in the bottom of the casserole dish; spread about half the can of refried beans and half the ground beef atop the tortilla layer. Sprinkle 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese over ground beef layer. Arrange 3 more tortillas over the cheese. Spread in the remaining 1/2 can refried beans and half the jar of taco sauce; sprinkle with 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese. Arrange 3 more tortillas and top with remaining ground beef and 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese. Top with last 3 tortillas and remaining 1/2 jar taco sauce.

Step 4: Bake in the preheated oven until top tortilla layer begins to brown, 20 to 25 minutes. Sprinkle 1 cup Colby-Jack cheese over lasagna.

Step 5: Turn on oven's broiler.

Step 6: Place lasagna under the broiler until Colby-Jack cheese is melted and bubbling, 3 to 5 minutes.
